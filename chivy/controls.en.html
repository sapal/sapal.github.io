How to move in menu?

KeyAction
Up and down arrowsChoose menu item
Left and right arrowsChange option
EnterEnter menu / change option
EscapeGo to previous menu / end game
It is also possible to use mouse to navigate menu.

How to control shapes?

Player 1

KeyAction
Up arrowGo up
Down arrowGo down
Left arrowGo left
Right arrowGo right
Right CtrlSwitch active shape
Right ShiftUse item / teleport

Player 2

KeyAction
WGo up
SGo down
AGo left
DGo right
QSwitch active shape
EUse item / teleport

Player 3

KeyAction
IGo up
KGo down
JGo left
LGo right
USwitch active shape
OUse item / teleport

Player 4

KeyAction
